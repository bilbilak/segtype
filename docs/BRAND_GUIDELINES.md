# Brand Guidelines

Welcome to the Brand Guidelines for **SegType™**. This document provides an overview of our brand identity, and outlines how to use our brand assets, including our logo, colors, typography, and other visual elements. Following these guidelines will help to maintain the strength and consistency of our brand across all media and platforms.

## Name

The project name is **SegType** — written in CamelCase with a capital S and capital T. Always render it as a single word.

- ✓ **_SegType_** (bold-italic when referring to the project itself)
- ✓ **SegType** (bold in headings)
- ✓ _SegType_ (italic in running text)
- ✗ segtype
- ✗ Segtype
- ✗ Seg-Type
- ✗ Seg Type

## Logo

The SegType logo is a stylised seven-segment glyph of the letter "S". It is distributed as a PNG asset at `.github/assets/segtype-icon.png`. Do not alter, recolor, or rotate the logo without prior approval.

## Attribution

When using SegType in your own projects, please attribute the project with a link to `https://github.com/bilbilak/segtype`.

## Trademark Notice

**SegType** is a trademark of **Bilbilak**. Unauthorised use of the name or logo for commercial purposes without permission is not permitted.
